# How to Install and Use IPTV Smarters Pro on Samsung Smart TV and LG Smart TV

IPTV Smarters Pro is a powerful streaming application that allows you to access thousands of live TV channels, movies, and TV shows on your smart TV. This comprehensive guide will walk you through the installation process for both Samsung Smart TV and LG Smart TV.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A Samsung Smart TV (Tizen OS) or LG Smart TV (webOS)
- An active IPTV subscription with Xtream Codes API credentials
- A stable internet connection
- Your IPTV service provider's login credentials (username, password, and server URL)

## Installation on Samsung Smart TV (Tizen OS)

### Step 1: Enable Developer Mode

1. Press the **Home** button on your Samsung Smart TV remote
2. Navigate to **Settings** > **System** > **Developer Mode**
3. Enable **Developer Mode** and note the IP address displayed

### Step 2: Install IPTV Smarters Pro

1. On your computer, download the IPTV Smarters Pro Tizen package (.wgt file)
2. Open Samsung Smart TV Developer Mode website using the IP address from Step 1
3. Upload the .wgt file and install it on your TV
4. The app will appear in your Smart Hub

### Step 3: Configure Your IPTV Service

1. Launch IPTV Smarters Pro from your Smart Hub
2. Select **Add User** or **Login**
3. Choose **Login with Xtream Codes API**
4. Enter your credentials:

> **Need help with login?** Check out our detailed guide on [How to Login Xtream Codes API on IPTV Smarters Pro](/how-to-login-xtream-codes-api-iptv-smarters-pro) for step-by-step instructions.

- **Username**: Your IPTV service username
- **Password**: Your IPTV service password
- **Server URL**: Your IPTV service server URL

5. Click **Add User** to save and connect

## Installation on LG Smart TV (webOS)

### Step 1: Enable Developer Mode

1. Press the **Home** button on your LG Smart TV remote
2. Go to **Settings** > **General** > **About This TV**
3. Find your TV's IP address
4. On your computer, visit the LG Developer website
5. Register your TV's IP address for developer mode

### Step 2: Install IPTV Smarters Pro

1. Download the IPTV Smarters Pro webOS package (.ipk file)
2. Use LG Developer tools to install the .ipk file on your TV
3. The app will be available in your LG Content Store or Apps section

### Step 3: Configure Your IPTV Service

1. Open IPTV Smarters Pro from your apps
2. Select **Add User** or **Login**
3. Choose **Login with Xtream Codes API**
4. Enter your IPTV credentials:
   - **Username**: Your IPTV service username
   - **Password**: Your IPTV service password
   - **Server URL**: Your IPTV service server URL
5. Save and start streaming

## Using IPTV Smarters Pro

Once installed and configured, you can:

- **Browse Live TV Channels**: Navigate through thousands of live TV channels organized by categories
- **Watch Movies & TV Shows**: Access your VOD (Video on Demand) library
- **Use EPG (Electronic Program Guide)**: View program schedules and information
- **Catch-up TV**: Watch previously aired programs (if supported by your provider)
- **Multi-screen Support**: Watch different channels on multiple devices simultaneously

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

**App won't install:**

- Ensure Developer Mode is properly enabled
- Check that your TV model is compatible
- Verify you have the correct package file for your TV's OS

**Can't connect to server:**

- Verify your internet connection
- Double-check your login credentials
- Contact your IPTV service provider to confirm server status

**Channels not loading:**

- Check your internet speed (minimum 10 Mbps recommended)
- Restart the app and try again
- Clear the app cache if available

**Playback issues:**

- Ensure your internet connection is stable
- Try a different channel to test
- Contact your IPTV provider for support
